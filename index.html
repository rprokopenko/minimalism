About us.
The BIG photo studio in Wroclaw is equipped with modern equipment and provides photographers with comfortable working conditions, which translates into the effects of photo sessions, castings and workshops.

We owe not only to professional equipment, but also to the large area of our studio and a number of large windows in the photo rooms.
